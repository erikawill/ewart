Hello! My name is Erika Williams. I enjoy learning about and creating various mediums of art such as graphic design, web design, and animation. I have always grown up around art - my mom draws, my little brother draws, and my aunt used to draw too - so it made sense for me to be an artist. I have knowledge with Adobe apps including Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and After Effects. I also love and appreciate feedback on my work - it's what helps me grow as an artist!
