ASSIGNMENT

Inspired by Paul Rand’s essay “Design and the Play Instinct,” design a book utilizing the images and text from the essay. Since I wanted to have fun with shapes and colors, I would say my design for this book has a “colorful structured-ness” to it. The design pertains to the layout of the triangles while the four colors (teal, purple, orange, and yellow) add a playful aspect throughout the book.
