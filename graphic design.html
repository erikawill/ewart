Postcard + Stamp
2022
CATEGORY
P
CLIENT
Personal Project
CONCEPT
A postcard and stamp made based off of the animal I chose for this project - the elephant. I chose the elephant as my animal for this project because it's one of my favorite animals. I thought the idea of centering my postcard around poaching would be important because it's one of the reasons why their population is decreasing.
SKETCHES
This project included two parts, each part containing three sketches. The first set are simple black and white drawings that include line, sketch, and stiple. The second set of drawings include heat, environment, and organic. The heat elephant resembles a heat map that shows the different levels of heat. My environment elephant has blue and green - the blue represents the water elephants drink from their trunk, the green represents the grass elephants walk on.
