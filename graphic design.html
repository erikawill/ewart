Historical Figure Project
2022
category
branding
client
personal project
software
concept
a stationary set that was created as a proposed design for a famous figure in history. I chose King Tut. the set includes a letterhead, business card, envelope, and a museum ticket (an addiotional item).
info
Logo: The logo is none other than King Tut. Instead of drawing a front facing view, I decided to make the logo a side profile to mimic hieroglyphics.
Letterhead: The letterhead is a simple design that includes King Tut's signature in the bottom left corner.
Envelope: The envelope mimics the letterhead's design, with the signature on the back.
Business Card: The business card mimics the letterhead's design, with the front having just the logo on it.
Museum Ticket: The museum ticket is an additional item that was added later in the project.
In The Mood For Love Poster
2023
category
entertainment
client
personal project
software
concept
a poster (and title sequence) inspired by the film "In The Mood For Love" by Wong Kar-Wai. For the poster, I wanted to keep it simple but also focus on different themes in the film - longing, separation, color. The background consists of one of Mrs. Chan's beautiful outfits - the blue and red rose quipao.
moodboards
for some of the titles, I put a clipping mask of images from the film in the Chinese characters, mainly to see how it would look. Also, I knew I wanted to include yellow and red because those two colors would pop up in a couple of scenes where Mrs. Chan and Mr. Chow were together.
