Movie Metaphor Poster

A poster for the movie "In The Mood For Love" by Wong Kar-Wai.

2023, Programs used: Illustrator, Photoshop

For this project, I had to choose a movie from a list given by the teacher and make a movie poster and title sequence for it. I chose "In The Mood For Love," a film by Wong Kar-Wai. I had to come up with moodboards [below] of some of the ideas I had.

My poster incorporates elements of the movie in it such as the red background, the yellow in the Chinese characters, and the clock. The red background mimics the red hallway in the movie while the gold represents the yellow light that shines down on them in a couple scnenes. I put the two characters Mrs. Chan [Maggie Cheung] and Mr. Chow [Tony Leung] on either side of the poster to symbolize the distance between the two. The clock, along with the movie title, separates the two. I added the clock because in the movie, there are multiple showings of the clock and a reference made to it by Mr. Chow. It represents how much time Mrs. Chan and Mr. Chow have left in their "relationship."
